News 2: India and Nepal Boost Power Sector Cooperation

India and Nepal have significantly strengthened their cooperation in the power sector.

Nepalese Minister of Energy, Kulman Ghising, met with India's Union Minister Manohar Lal, leading to the signing of key agreements between POWERGRID and the Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA).

These agreements facilitate the implementation of major cross-border transmission systems, including the Inaruwa (Nepal) - New Purnea (India) 400 kV and Lamki (Dododhara) (Nepal) - Bareilly (India) 400kV systems, enhancing regional grid connectivity.
